BLACK CAT CAFE

LOWER EAST SIDE

172 Rivington St.

Based in the lower east side, Black Cat Cafe features works of art and cozy drinks for all to enjoy. The atmosphere is warm. A variety of people cram around the small space to chat or catchup on work. Two workers hustle behind the counter fullfilling drink orders and yelling out customers names. The smell of butter and burnt cheese fill the stagnant air every once in a while.
