plantshed

PlantShed is a beautiful integration of seasonal florals with the comfort of cozy drinks. People are attracted by the beautfiul arrangements sitting outside the café.

There was pottery and cute home decor on shelves in the back of the café. Nobody was taking time to look at the products or roam around the store.

The plants look lively and well groomed, a nice escape from the outdoor heat and the city scape. Lovely shades of green line the walls. My favorite were a batch of succulents.
